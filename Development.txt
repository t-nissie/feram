= How to develop feram
GNU autotools are required for development.

Get current source tree with svn(1).
 $ svn checkout https://loto.svn.sourceforge.net/svnroot/loto
 $ cd loto/feram/trunk/
 $ ls -l
 $ autoreconf -v
 $ automake --add-missing
 $ autoreconf -v
 $ ls -l
 $ ./configure
 $ cd src
 $ make
 $ make feram_mpi

Useful svn commands:
 $ svn update
 $ svn stat
 $ svn diff
 $ svn log

How to execute:
 $ ./feram foo1.feram foo2.feram foo3.feram &
 $ mpiexec -np 3 ./feram_mpi foo1.feram foo2.feram foo3.feram &
