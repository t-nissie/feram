Heating-up and cooling-down simulations of ferroelectric capacitors

Animations of holizontal slices of heating-up and cooling-down simulations for BaTiO3 thin-film capacitors with short-circuited electrodes under 1% in-plane biaxial compressive strain. The +z-polarized and −z-polarized sites are denoted by red open squares and blue filled squares, respectively. Used supercell sizes are Lx×Ly×Lz = 40×40×2(l+d) .
